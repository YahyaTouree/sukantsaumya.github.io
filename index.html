Parking Management System

FlutterFirebaseGoogle Maps APIStripe

The Challenge

Urban drivers face constant frustration finding available parking. The goal was to create a seamless mobile solution to locate, reserve, and pay for parking spots in real-time, reducing congestion and stress.

My Process & Contribution

I architected and developed a full-featured Flutter application from the ground up. This involved implementing secure user authentication with Firebase, integrating the Google Maps API for a live view of parking availability, and building a robust booking and payment system with Stripe and PayPal APIs.

The Outcome & Results

The result is a comprehensive platform that not only helps drivers but also provides an admin panel for parking lot owners to manage spots and bookings efficiently. The app successfully streamlines the entire parking lifecycle, from search to payment.

Music Player App

FlutterClean ArchitectureRetrofitSQLite

The Challenge

To develop a high-performance music player that offers both online streaming and offline playback capabilities, while maintaining a clean, scalable, and maintainable codebase.

My Process & Contribution

I adopted Clean Architecture principles to ensure a decoupled and testable app structure. I integrated online music APIs using Retrofit for seamless streaming and implemented an SQLite database for robust offline storage and playback. ExoPlayer was utilized for its efficiency and advanced playback features.

The Outcome & Results

The final product is a feature-rich music application that provides a smooth and reliable user experience. The architectural choices ensure the app is easy to scale and maintain, demonstrating a strong understanding of mobile development best practices.

Visual Read Assist Device

PythonRaspberry PiOCRTTS

The Challenge

To bridge the accessibility gap for visually impaired individuals by creating a portable, affordable device that can read printed text aloud in real-time.

My Process & Contribution

I engineered a solution using a Raspberry Pi 4 and Pi Camera. I implemented the Tesseract OCR engine for accurate image-to-text conversion and integrated a Text-to-Speech (TTS) module for clear auditory output. A key focus was designing a simple, user-friendly interface with physical controls for playback.

The Outcome & Results

The project resulted in a functional and effective assistive device that empowers visually impaired users by granting them greater independence. The device provides real-time text-to-speech conversion and includes features for digital backup, showcasing a practical application of embedded systems and AI for social good.
